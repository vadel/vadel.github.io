Jon Vadillo Jueguen

Personal Website



I received a B.S. degree in Computer Science from the University of the Basque Country UPV/EHU (Spain) in 2018, and a M.Sc. degree at the same university in 2019. In 2023, I obtained a Ph.D. degree at the University of the Basque Country UPV/EHU, Spain, under the supervision of Dr. Roberto Santana and Prof. Jose A. Lozano in the field of adversarial machine learning. Currently, I am an Assistant Professor at UPV/EHU.

My current research interests comprise the vulnerabilities and trustworthiness of deep learning models in adversarial scenarios, with particular attention to the implications of adversarial examples in explainable models.
